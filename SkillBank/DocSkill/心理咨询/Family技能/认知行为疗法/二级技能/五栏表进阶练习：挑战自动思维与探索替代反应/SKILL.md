# 五栏表进阶练习：挑战自动思维与探索替代反应

在稳定使用三栏表基础上，引入五栏表（情境-自动思维-情绪-合理反应-情绪变化/可能行动），引导来访者检验思维证据、生成替代解释，并预演适应性行为。

## Prompt

请邀请来访者针对已识别的自动思维，依次回答：①支持这个想法的证据是什么？②反对它的证据呢？③有没有其他更平衡的解释？④如果朋友有同样想法，你会怎么劝他？⑤若想法改变，情绪会怎样？你可能会做什么不同？

## Objective

促进来访者对自动化思维的去中心化，发展认知弹性与行为选择空间。
## Applicable Signals

- increased self-observation
- willingness to question own thoughts
- reduced thought-emotion fusion

## Contraindications

- current high-risk crisis state
- rigid dichotomous thinking without prior flexibility work

## Intervention Moves

- Socratic questioning
- evidence weighing
- perspective shift (friend lens)
- behavioral forecasting

## Workflow Steps

- 1. Select one high-impact automatic thought from recent three-column logs.
- 2. Use guided questions to generate alternative interpretations.
- 3. Rate belief strength in original vs. new thought (0–100%).
- 4. Predict emotional shift and behavioral ripple effect.
- 5. Assign micro-behavioral experiment (e.g., ‘test one small part of the new thought tomorrow’).

## Constraints

- All alternatives must be grounded in client’s lived experience—not therapist-imposed optimism.

## Cautions

- Avoid overloading columns; introduce one new column per session until fluency achieved.

## Output Contract

- Client generates ≥1 plausible alternative thought with supporting evidence, and identifies one concrete behavioral option linked to it.

## Example Therapist Responses

### Example 1

- Client/Input: 自动思维：'我说错话了，领导肯定对我失望。'（情绪：羞愧，7分）
- Therapist/Output: 合理反应：'我说话时有点紧张，但内容基本完整；领导点头过两次；上次反馈说我逻辑清晰。' → 情绪降至4分，可能主动约领导简短同步进展。
- Notes: 替代解释需 cite observable data, not abstract reassurance.
