# 三栏表引导与情绪-思维-行为联结教学

在来访者情绪激动、思路混乱时，使用三栏表（情境-自动思维-情绪）进行结构化教学，帮助其建立情绪、自动思维与行为之间的认知联结。

## Prompt

请和来访者一起回顾一个近期引发强烈情绪的具体情境；引导其识别当时闪现的自动思维，并标注对应的情绪类型与强度（0–10分）；最后简要说明该思维可能如何影响其后续行为。

## Objective

帮助来访者初步识别并命名自动思维，建立情绪-思维-行为的认知三角联结。
## Applicable Signals

- verbal emotional dysregulation
- cognitive disorganization
- low metacognitive awareness

## Contraindications

- acute suicidality without safety plan in place
- severe dissociation or reality testing impairment

## Intervention Moves

- normalize emotional reactivity
- label the 'automatic' quality of thoughts
- anchor emotion to concrete situation

## Workflow Steps

- 1. Elicit a recent emotionally charged incident.
- 2. Guide naming of dominant emotion and intensity (0–10).
- 3. Probe for the first thought that popped up — not interpretation, just verbatim.
- 4. Explicitly link: 'That thought happened *right as* you felt X — before any action.'
- 5. Co-label the pattern: 'This is what we call an automatic thought.'

## Constraints

- Must use present-tense, non-judgmental language; avoid 'should', 'irrational', or 'distorted' at this stage.

## Cautions

- Do not move to challenge before consistent identification is demonstrated; premature reframing risks alliance rupture.

## Output Contract

- Client can independently complete a filled three-column table for one recent event, with clear separation of columns and plausible content in each.

## Example Therapist Responses

### Example 1

- Client/Input: 来访者说：'昨天开会发言后，我整晚睡不着，心跳很快，觉得自己肯定说错了话，领导会对我失望。'
- Therapist/Output: 情境：昨天开会发言后；自动思维：'我肯定说错了话，领导会对我失望'；情绪：焦虑（8分）
- Notes: 咨询师未修正或 dispute 思维内容，仅协助分离与标注。
