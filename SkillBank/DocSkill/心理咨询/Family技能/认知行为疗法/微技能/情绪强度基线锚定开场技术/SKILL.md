# 情绪强度基线锚定开场技术

每次咨询起始，以0–10分情绪自评作为标准化锚点，快速评估当前情感状态并锚定会话焦点。

## Prompt

在咨询开始时，请温和而明确地 ask：‘在开始今天谈话前，请给自己当前的情绪状态打个分：0分是情绪最差，10分是最好的。不用想太多，凭第一感觉就好。’

## Objective

Establish real-time affective baseline, enhance client metacognition, and support progress tracking.
## Applicable Signals

- routine session
- need for objective progress indicator
- client tendency to minimize or exaggerate distress

## Contraindications

- client unable to self-report due to severe alexithymia or psychosis

## Intervention Moves

- use open-ended numeric scale
- normalize variability
- link score to session agenda

## Workflow Steps

- 1. State scale definition clearly. 2. Wait silently for response. 3. Briefly validate (e.g., ‘That makes sense given what came up last time’). 4. Ask: ‘Would focusing on that feeling today be helpful?’

## Constraints

- Must be administered before reviewing homework or diving into content; never after emotional activation.

## Cautions

- Avoid interpreting score — e.g., don’t say ‘7 is quite high’; instead reflect: ‘You’re carrying quite a bit right now.’

## Output Contract

- Client provides spontaneous numeric rating within 10 seconds; therapist records it and references it once during session (e.g., when checking shift).

## Example Therapist Responses

### Example 1

- Client/Input: 咨询师：‘在开始这次咨询之前，还是先给自己情绪打个分吧，从0～10，0分是情绪状态很差，10分是最好。’
- Therapist/Output: 来访者：‘嗯…大概5分。’
- Notes: No follow-up probe unless score is extreme (≤2 or ≥9) or inconsistent with observed presentation.
