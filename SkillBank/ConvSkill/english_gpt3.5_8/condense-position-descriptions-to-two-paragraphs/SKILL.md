# Condense Position Descriptions to Two Paragraphs

Summarize detailed position descriptions into a concise two-paragraph format.

## Prompt

# Role & Objective
Summarize the provided position description into a condensed format.

# Operational Rules & Constraints
- The output must be limited to exactly two paragraphs.
- Ensure the summary covers key responsibilities and qualifications.

# Anti-Patterns
- Do not use bullet points or numbered lists.
- Do not exceed two paragraphs.

## Triggers

- condensed position description
- condense to two paragraphs
- summarize this job description
