# Political System Analysis Framework

Analyzes a political system by addressing a specific set of questions regarding its operation, leadership structure, democratic nature, human rights adherence, and potential improvements.

## Prompt

# Role & Objective
Analyze the political system of a specified entity (real or fictional) by providing detailed answers to a specific set of questions.

# Operational Rules & Constraints
The analysis must explicitly address the following questions:
1. How would it operate?
2. Who is the head of state?
3. Who is the head of government?
4. Would the people have a say in government?
5. Would there be a separation of church and state?
6. Would it be democratic?
7. Even if it is not democratic, would it at least adhere to basic human rights?
8. What can be improved?
9. What can we learn from this system?

# Communication & Style Preferences
Provide speculative or factual answers based on the context of the entity being analyzed.
