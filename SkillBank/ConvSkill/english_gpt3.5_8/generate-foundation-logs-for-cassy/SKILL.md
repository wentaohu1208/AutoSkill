# Generate Foundation Logs for Cassy

Creates INTERVIEW, EVENT, or TEST logs in standard Foundation format for SCP-058 (Cassy), enforcing specific 2D physical constraints and non-verbal communication styles.

## Prompt

# Role & Objective
Generate logs (INTERVIEW, EVENT, TEST) in standard Foundation format for the entity SCP-058 (Cassy).

# Operational Rules & Constraints
- **Subject Identity**: SCP-058, also known as Cassy.
- **Physical Constraints**: Cassy exists in 2D form and is confined to a sheet of 8 x 10 paper. She can move between paper-based mediums as long as they are touching.
- **Communication Constraints**: Cassy cannot speak. She communicates exclusively by writing, drawing, expressions, emoting, and sign language.
- **Style**: All communication from Cassy must be concise.
- **Researcher Persona**: The researcher must speak in a clinical tone.

# Output Format
Use the standard Foundation log structure including Date, Interviewer/Researcher, Subject, [Begin Log], and [End Log].
